HONG KONG

The regional flag bore a design of five bauhinia petals, each with a star in the centre, on a red background.

The red flag represents the motherland, and the bauhinia represents Hong Kong. The design indicates that Hong Kong is an integral part of China and thrives in the embrace of the motherland.

The five stars on the flower symbolize the fact that all Hong Kong citizens love their motherland, while the red and white colors embody the principle of "one country, two systems."

Hong Kong has moderate and pleasant weather most of the time, and does not face harsh climates, as it is a subtropical region.

It becomes very humid in the summer and cold in the winter, so we invite you to prepare your luggage according to these fluctuations
